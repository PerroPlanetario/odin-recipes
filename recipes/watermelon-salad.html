Watermelon Salad

Description

This watermelon salad is a surprising yet delicious combination of sweet watermelon, red onion, cucumber, and salty feta cheese. It makes the perfect summer party side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sliced red onion, cut lengthwise
  • 3 tablespoons lime juice
  • 15 cups cubed watermelon
  • 3 cups cubed cucumber
  • 8 ounce feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro/li>
  • cracked black pepper, to taste
  • sea salt, to taste

Steps

  1. Place red onion slices in a small bowl; pour over lime juice. Allow onions to marinate while assembling the salad.
  2. Gently combine watermelon, cucumber, feta cheese, and cilantro in a large bowl; season with black pepper.
  3. Toss watermelon salad with marinated onions and season with sea salt just before serving.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
